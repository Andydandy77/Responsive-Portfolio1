About Me

Transitioning from the semiconductor industry with a Bachelors of Science in Electrical Engineering and a strong background in object oriented programming, I aim to become a software engineer focusing in full stack development and data analytics. As a passionate and unrelenting student, I currently am enrolled in UW’s Coding Bootcamp, learning the MERN development stack, all while working as a full time electrical engineer. I quickly pick up new algorithm concepts and do not hesitate to apply them in projects. In the next coming years I strive to expand into machine learning development, for I believe it will be the single most important field in the software industry. This website displays several of my strongest projects showcasing the newest technologies I have learned.
