About Me

I have worked in the semiconductor industry with Cypress Semiconductor for the last year as an Electrical Design Engineer, focusing on Analog IC design. Recently, however, I feel drawn to the possiblities of innovation in software and web development.

I also have a strong interest in learning more about deep learning, neural networks and artificial intelligence. This displays several of my strongest projects showcasing my skills. I also enjoy hiking, climbing, cooking, playing guitar, piano, bass, drums and singing. My secret passion is to become a indie rockstar.
